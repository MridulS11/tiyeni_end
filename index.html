How do I book a ride?
Booking a ride is simple! Just enter your pickup and drop-off locations, choose your preferred ride type, and confirm your booking. You can book through our website or mobile app.

We accept multiple payment options, including credit/debit cards, digital wallets (Google Pay, Apple Pay), and cash payments for select rides.

You can cancel your ride through the app before the driver arrives. Cancellation fees may apply if the driver is already on the way. Check our cancellation policy for details.
